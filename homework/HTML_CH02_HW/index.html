Gaming

My favorite thing to do in my spare time is play video games. I usually play PC games that I download from Steam, but I also own a Nintendo Switch, which is usually for when I am away from home and wish to play portably. While I prefer a keyboard for first person games, I will use a controller for platformers or simulation games. Sandbox and roguelikes are my personal favorite genres of games.
